What You Can Expect at the RRRide

Ride Routes

Choose your distance for the day: 15, 40 or 80 miles. Our routes take you along scenic tree-lined roads with gentle rolling hills, beginning and ending at the Farmer’s Daughter on RT. 138 in Kingston.

Rally Crew

The amazing RRR volunteers will be stationed and riding along the course to provide nutrition, first aid, mechanical support, and an emotional boost. They will be championing you along the way.

Refreshments

We provide food and fun before, during, and after the ride. You’ll enjoy music and cheer at the finish line, so be sure to invite your friends and families to celebrate your accomplishment.

Two Registration Options

Fundraising Riders: $30 registration, with $300 fundraising commitment ($40 after February 1); Non-fundraising Riders: $200 registration, with no fundraising commitment ($250 After February 1)
